some stuff about me

Hello there, again! I'm Vlad, a 14-year-old from Romania! I'm really into design, especially creating awesome wallpapers that captivate people's imagination. It's like painting with pixels and colors, and I absolutely love it!

I also have a keen interest in photography. The world is full of beauty, and I find joy in capturing it through my camera lens. But here's the thing - I'm not the type of photographer who travels to far-off places to take pictures. Instead, I love finding beauty in the everyday things around me, even in the comfort of my own home. It's like a treasure hunt for the perfect shot, right in my own little world.

Designing and photography are my outlets for self-expression. They allow me to share my unique perspective and feelings with the world. I'm always exploring new techniques, learning from other creatives, and collaborating with fellow enthusiasts. There's something magical about connecting with others who share the same passions as me.
